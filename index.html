Snapdragon Inn

Welcome !

Nestled among rolling vineyards and quiet country lanes, the Snapdragon Inn is a place to slow down and savor what matters. Designed with couples in mind, our bed and breakfast offers a refined yet welcoming escape where the pace is gentle, the atmosphere is intimate, and every detail invites connection.

Here, we trade screens for sunsets and notifications for long, unhurried conversations. Mornings begin with thoughtfully prepared breakfasts, and days unfold with curated wine tours, scenic walks, or simply time spent together in peaceful surroundings.

Whether you’re celebrating something special or just seeking a moment away, the Snapdragon Inn is your retreat for romance, relaxation, and the simple luxury of being present.

Accommodations

At Snapdragon Inn, each of our eight rooms offers its own quiet kind of romance—thoughtfully designed for rest, connection, and the simple beauty of Paso Robles. From the cozy Garden and Lavender Rooms to the light-filled Willow and Meadow, every space invites you to slow down and settle in. The Vineyard and Sunset Rooms bring wine country to your window, with views that linger long after dusk. For something more indulgent, the Rose Balcony Room and Snapdragon Suite offer private outdoor space, sweeping vistas, and an extra touch of magic. Whichever you choose, you’ll find comfort, character, and just enough charm to make leaving the hardest part.
